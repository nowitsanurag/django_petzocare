Frequently Asked
Questions

How do I book an appointment for a virtual pet care consultation?

To book an appointment for a virtual pet care consultation, please follow these steps:

  1. Go to our website and navigate to the "Virtual Vet Visits" section.
  2. Click on the "Book an Appointment" button.
  3. Select the type of consultation you need (e.g. routine checkup, behavior consultation, etc.).
  4. Choose a date and time that works for you.
  5. Fill out the appointment request form with your pet's information and any specific concerns you have.
  6. Submit the form, and we'll get back to you as soon as possible to confirm your appointment.
What types of pet health concerns can be addressed during a virtual consultation?
  1. Routine checkups and preventive care: Regular virtual consultations can help ensure your pet is in good health and catch any potential health issues early on.
  2. Behavioral issues: Our veterinarians can provide guidance and advice on common behavioral issues such as anxiety, aggression, and house training.
  3. Skin and coat issues: Virtual consultations can help address skin and coat issues such as itching, hair loss, and hot spots.
  4. Digestive issues: Our veterinarians can help diagnose and treat digestive issues such as vomiting, diarrhea, and constipation.
  5. Respiratory issues: Virtual consultations can help address respiratory issues such as coughing, sneezing, and breathing difficulties.
  6. Joint and mobility issues: Our veterinarians can provide guidance and advice on managing joint pain and mobility issues, as well as prescribe medication if necessary.
What happens if my pet requires in-person treatment or tests?
If your pet requires in-person treatment or tests, our veterinarians may recommend that you bring your pet to a physical clinic for an examination and diagnostic tests. In some cases, virtual consultations may not provide enough information to diagnose or treat your pet's health condition. Our team will work with you to determine the best course of action for your pet's health and well-being.
How do I pay for the virtual pet care consultation and what are the fees?
We accept payment for virtual pet care consultations through our secure online payment system. Our fees for virtual consultations may vary depending on the type of consultation and the length of the session. Please check our website for the latest information on our fees and payment options. We also offer discounted rates for bundled consultation packages and subscription plans. Please contact us for more information.
